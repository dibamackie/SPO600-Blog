Welcome to My 6502 Programming Journey

This blog serves as an introduction to my exploration of the 6502 microprocessor. The 6502 is a classic CPU, used in many famous systems such as the NES and Commodore 64. Through this journey, I will share my learning experiences, code snippets, and insights into assembly programming.

Why 6502?

The 6502 architecture is simple yet powerful, making it a great starting point for understanding low-level programming. By working with the 6502, I hope to:

  • Develop a deeper understanding of how computers work at the hardware level.
  • Enhance my debugging and optimization skills.
  • Gain experience with assembly language programming.

What to Expect


This blog will feature:

  • Detailed explanations of lab exercises and projects.
  • Code snippets with step-by-step walkthroughs.
  • Performance analysis and optimization techniques.
  • Reflections on mistakes and lessons learned.
